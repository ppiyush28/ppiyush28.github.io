About Me

I am currently working as an Senior Data Scientist at Citi India . My team is based in India and we primarily work on solving challenging NLP problems at scale to take AI/NLP to the next level. Previously, I was working at Citi as a Senior Data Scientist where we were leveraging AI to make the transactions world a smarter and secure place. In my corporate experience, I have been involved both in incorporating AI and Machine Learning in product development (leading to product launches/patents) as well as pure research work (publishing papers at AI conferences).

I pursuing my Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) from Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Nagpur. My Research advisor are Dr. Pooja Jain & Dr. Nidhi Lal. I worked on Machine Learning, NLP and its application in the BFSI industry. Previously I had also worked with Credit Suisse, Pune on Trade data Prediction and HSBC on Fraud Detection, Credit card and Scorecards
